Semi-solid Casting Applications

Semi-solid casting is used for high-integrity aluminium and magnesium parts where low porosity, precision surface quality and mechanical performance are important. Typical applications include hydraulic brake valves, wheels, brake master cylinders, oil filter housings, door pillars, steering control arms, engine brackets, cylinder blocks and support structures.
